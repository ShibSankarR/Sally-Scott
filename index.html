About
Sally Scott

25+ years of experience in complex analysis, investigation, determination/advice and conflict resolution.

My career as a lawyer has awarded me with a deep knowledge of the legal system and a unique and highly specialised set of skills for workplace investigations. As a result of a background in complex litigation in addition to my analytical nature, I have spent many years honing the very skills which have propelled me as a highly adept legal investigator. Engagement is at the heart of an investigation. I posses an ability to quickly and genuinely connect with people in a caring manner which enables me to conduct in-depth and less stressful investigations.

I am an empathetic listener who builds confidence through support and investing effort in explaining the investigation to the complainant and respondent. This gives a sense of empowerment and assures that everyone feels heard and has been given their chance to state their positions. I am assertive when the situation calls for it, assuring all evidence is gathered in a professional manner. Organisations engage me as a trusted expert in the field, who guides them thoughtfully through the process. I am equally firm in my obligations as a lawyer and allow no bias to interrupt my conclusions.

Ethos
& Approach

Lateral thiking
& deep investigator

A natural analytic nature allows me to quickly identify and hone in on key issues of a case, which in turn leads to clear legal outcomes. I have an innate thirst for information which has made me an innovative thinker, pursing paths which aren’t always considered in conventional investigations. My commercial litigation background is extremely helpful providing a unique skill set. It has given me a breadth of investigation and analysis experience, enabling me to address the investigations from numerous angles and legal perspectives. This often yields vital information which aids the investigation.

Establishing a rapport through
respect & Care for others

I am conscious of the psychological wellbeing of all involved in the investigation. My investigations are conducted in a manner which is respectful and thoughtful of each interviewee. I am aware of how stressful investigations can be and do my upmost to assure that everyone is given a safe environment where they feel they can share their story. This allows for a free flowing exchange of information, reducing any resistance to communicate all facets of the issue. My care for the parties involved extends beyond the case which in turn minimises morale issues and stress. The mental health of the individual and the workplace must be considered during an investigation.

Objectivity

I am an unbiased adjudicator analysing the information to result in a fair and legally sound set of findings. I look at each case from an expert perspective, creating clarity in the final reporting. Interviewing is a fact finding mission and if the facts don’t point towards a legal outcome, I have the confidence and ethics to recommend an investigation be terminated (where possible) and present an alternate way to resolve the matter. I review all evidence with impartiality and present a fair and legal set of findings for successful case outcomes.

Legal Outcome

I run a tight ship in terms of adhering to principles of natural justice of an investigation and complying with rules. I invest deep thought in analysing what the issues are, and the best approaches to try to resolve them, instead of just mindlessly following procedure. In this way, I am a creative in my analysis, allowing legally sound outcomes to unfold.
